Education

I am a masters student at UCL studying linguistics with a specialisation in pragmatics. Prior to my masters degree, I graduated from the University of Brighton with a first class honours in linguistics.

Music

In my spare time I produce ambient electronic music. I also produce dense sprawling chiptune-esque tracks. This production occurs under a few monikers but all can be found here. I have in the past operated an online record label (netlabel) called Question Records. It is currently on indefinite hiatus whilst I focus on the more present and important parts of life.

Academia

I am enthralled by the pragmatics of emotion, music perception and the more cognitive aspects and research in lingustics. See below for a non-exhaustive list of my academic interests:

  • emotion (philosophical, cognitive and pragmatic)
  • music perception
  • music and emotion
  • relevance theory
  • modularity of mind and pragmatics
